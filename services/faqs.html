How do I create Razer Synapse account?

  1. Launch Razer Synapse 3.

How do I register my Razer Product in Synapse 3?

  1. Launch Razer Synapse 3.
  2. Click “REGISTER YOUR RAZER PRODUCT”.
  3. Sign in with your Razer ID.
  4. Click “PRODUCTS” > “REGISTER A NEW PRODUCT”.
